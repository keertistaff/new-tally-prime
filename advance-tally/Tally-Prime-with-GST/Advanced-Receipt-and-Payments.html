Objective

Learn how to:

  • Enable GST in TallyPrime.
  • Create all required masters.
  • Record GST Advance Receipts.
  • Adjust GST for advances.
  • Handle sales against advances.
  • Handle cancelled advances and refunds.

Part 1: Company GST Configuration

Before recording any GST transaction, GST must be activated in the company.

Step 1: Enable GST

Path:

Gateway of Tally → F11 (Features) → Statutory & Taxation

Enable

  • Enable Goods and Services Tax (GST) = Yes
  • Set/Alter GST Details = Yes

Enter the required details carefully.

Common Step for All Scenarios – Recording Advance Receipt

Path:

Receipt Voucher → Alt + J (Stat Adjustment)

Select Advance Receipt.

Entry

  • Select Bank/Cash Account.
  • Select Customer Ledger.
  • Enter Advance Amount = ₹10,000.

Tally calculates:

  • Taxable Value = ₹8,474.58
  • GST = ₹1,525.42

Scenario 1 – Advance Received and Sale Made in Same Month

Overview

When the advance and sales invoice are recorded within the same GST period, no GST adjustment journal voucher is required.

Create Sales Invoice

Path: Sales Voucher

Item Qty Rate Amount
Book 20 Nos ₹500 ₹10,000
Ledger Amount
CGST @ 9% ₹900
SGST @ 9% ₹900
Total GST ₹1,800

Invoice Value = ₹11,800

Adjust Advance

  • Select Against Reference.
  • Select Advance Reference.
  • Enter ₹10,000.

Balance Due: ₹11,800 – ₹10,000 = ₹1,800

Verification

  • GSTR-1 → GST Liability = ₹1,800
  • Balance Sheet → Current Liabilities = ₹1,800

Important Note

  • No Increase Tax Liability Entry Required.
  • No Decrease Tax Liability Entry Required.
  • Advance is adjusted directly in the sales invoice.

Scenario 2 – Advance Received in One Month and Sale Made in Next Month

Overview

GST must be paid in the month in which the advance is received.

  1. Increase Tax Liability.
  2. Create Sales Invoice in next month.
  3. Decrease Tax Liability.

Step 1 – Increase Tax Liability

Path: Journal Voucher → Alt + J

GST → Increase in Tax Liability → On Account of Advance Receipt

Ledger Dr Cr
Tax on Advance 1,525.42
CGST 762.71
SGST 762.71

Step 2 – Create Sales Invoice in Next Month

Create invoice normally and adjust advance through Against Reference.

Step 3 – Decrease Tax Liability

Path: Journal Voucher → Alt + J

GST → Decrease in Tax Liability → Sales Against Advance Receipts

Ledger Dr Cr
CGST 762.71
SGST 762.71
Tax on Advance 1,525.42

GST already paid on advance gets adjusted against GST on the sales invoice.

Scenario 3 – Advance Received but Order Cancelled

Overview

Customer pays advance. GST liability is created. Later customer cancels the order. Advance is refunded. GST liability must be reversed.

Step 1 – Record Advance Receipt

Receipt Voucher → Alt + J → Advance Receipt

Step 2 – Increase Tax Liability

Ledger Dr Cr
Tax on Advance 1,525.42
CGST 762.71
SGST 762.71

Step 3 – Refund Advance

Path: Payment Voucher → Alt + J

  • Select Refund Against Advance Receipt.
  • Select Original Advance Reference.
  • Enter Refund Amount.
  • Save.

Step 4 – Reverse GST Liability

Path: Journal Voucher → Alt + J

GST → Decrease in Tax Liability → Cancellation of Advance Receipts

Ledger Dr Cr
CGST 762.71
SGST 762.71
Tax on Advance 1,525.42

Result

Advance is refunded. GST liability created earlier is completely reversed. No GST remains payable on the cancelled transaction.

Complete Flow Summary

Scenario 1

Advance Receipt → Sales Invoice → Adjust Advance → Done

Scenario 2

Advance Receipt → Increase Tax Liability → Sales Invoice → Decrease Tax Liability (Sales Against Advance Receipts) → Done

Scenario 3

Advance Receipt → Increase Tax Liability → Refund of Advance → Decrease Tax Liability (Cancellation of Advance Receipts) → Done

These three scenarios cover the complete GST treatment of Advance Receipts, Advance Adjustments, and Advance Refunds in TallyPrime.
